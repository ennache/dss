The Problem

The problem we identified

Considering the precarious state of the medical system in Romania, we are often forced to turn to private medical services. Some companies prefer to be cryptic about the rates charged for certain services or make it hard to find them out. Without transparency on a certain market, the customer suffers the most. Often, the customer is not aware of the market price and cannot make a suitable choice for its needs.

If in some situations a client has time to do a thorough research, when it comes to a medical service, many times, the client is tempted to choose the clinic that is recommended by the doctor or the clinic which is best marketed.This urgency of the situation gives an unfair advantage to clinics that can raise their prices, far above the market level or the value of the service.

The Solution

The solution we tought of

If a clinic is too expensive for a client, he can aggravate his condition, postponing the investigation. The customer assumes that the price that is first told represents the market average and knows that he cannot afford it.

This is where our platform comes in.The platform aims to centralize information from the websites of medical clinics regarding the services they offer and their prices. Thus, the client does not have to search through 5 different websites or talk to each call center of the clinics in order to find out a price range for his needs.

The client can compare the prices and the doctors who offer these services and get in direct contact with the clinic he considers suitable. In addition to facilitating the process of choosing a clinic, our platform can also force a ceiling and a stabilization tothe market, so that the competition is beneficial for the users of the services.

Customer Segment

The main beneficiaries of the platform would be the patients who will be able to enjoy a transparent and competitive offer of medical services. Private clinics will also be able to market their services through the platform.

Competition

A platform for comparing services doesn't exist yet, this means we will lay the foundation of a new niche. However, our scope overlaps with three other competitors:

  • Compari.ro, a platform which you can use to compare the price of different products from partner stores. It is designed for people who are looking for products, not services.
  • Compariorice.ro, idem
  • Stailer.ro, beauty services comparison platform only

Our advantage over the competition

  • A service evaluation system focused on user needs (quality-price ratio, rating, location, etc.)
  • Users have at their fingertips a multitude of services just a click away
  • An extensive number of services and collaborators.
  • Our search platform also makes it easy to schedule your chosen service.
  • Promotes contractors who provide quality service at a reasonable price.

Key Metrics

The solution offers a variety of key metrics. Some tend to be more volatile and some impact the revenue stream in a higher degree. Here are some of the key metrics:

  • Platform web traffic
  • The number of registered clinics
  • Customer conversion rate
  • Contacted clinics conversion rate
  • Platform reviews
  • User activity

Cost Structure

The operational costs spread as follows:

  • Cloud/Hardware maintainance (fixed)
  • Licenses (fixed)

Revenue Streams

The income stream is tight coupled with some key metrics and is composed from smaller revenue streams as follows:

  • Percentage-based revenue on customer conversion. The clinics will pay a variable ammount every time a user is converted into a customer based on the weight of conversion (how much the user we brought has spent).
  • Revenue from joining fees paid by clinics.
  • Ad based revenue from users
  • Revenue from paid promotions
  • Revenue from maintenance fees. A clinic will have to pay an amount once in a while (ex every 6 months) in order for it to stay on the platform.
