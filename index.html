The Problem

The problem we identified

We often need or choose to use private medical services and those who are offering these services prefer to be cryptic about the rates charged or make it hard to find them out. This leads to a lack of transparency in the market, which gives an unfair advantage to clinics that can raise their prices, far above the market level or the value of the service.

The Solution

The solution we tought of

Our solution consists in centralizing information from the websites of medical clinics regarding the services they offer and their prices. Through this platform the client can compare the prices and the doctors who offer these services and get in direct contact with the clinic he considers suitable for him.

Customer Segment

Our clients are mostly people from the urban areas, with access to the Internet and the necessary knowledge to use it, who are in the situation of using a medical service offered by a private clinic, but which is not covered by a subscription.

Competition

A platform for comparing services doesn't exist yet, this means we will lay the foundation of a new niche. However, our scope overlaps with three other competitors:

  • doc.ro
  • cautdoctor.ro
  • doclandia.ro
  • site-ul clinicilor
  • asigurator

Our advantage over the competition

  • A service evaluation system focused on user needs (quality-price ratio, rating, location, etc.)
  • Users have at their fingertips a multitude of services just a click away
  • An extensive number of services and collaborators.
  • Our search platform also makes it easy to schedule your chosen service.
  • Promotes contractors who provide quality service at a reasonable price.

Key Metrics

The solution offers a variety of key metrics. Some tend to be more volatile and some impact the revenue stream in a higher degree. Here are some of the key metrics:

  • The number of registered clinics
  • Customer conversion rate
  • Contacted clinics conversion rate
  • Platform reviews

Cost Structure

The operational costs spread as follows:

  • Cloud/Hardware maintainance (fixed)
  • Marketing (variable)

Revenue Streams

The income stream is tight coupled with some key metrics and is composed from smaller revenue streams as follows:

  • Ad based revenue from users
  • Revenue from paid promotions
